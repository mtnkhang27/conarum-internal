## Role Descriptions

### Player (PredictionUser)
Players are the core participants of the platform. Their primary goal is to make accurate predictions and compete for the top spots on the leaderboard. They have access to all prediction and betting features, as well as their personal history and profile.

### Admin (PredictionAdmin)
Administrators are responsible for the smooth operation of the platform. They manage the master data for tournaments, matches, and teams, and ensure that results are entered accurately and on time. They have full control over the platform's configuration and can manage all user accounts.
