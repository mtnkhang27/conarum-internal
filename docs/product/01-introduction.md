# Product Introduction

## Overview
**Conarum Prediction** is an internal sports prediction platform designed for company employees to engage with major sporting tournaments, such as the FIFA World Cup 2026. The platform fosters a competitive and social environment where participants can predict match outcomes, exact scores, and tournament champions to win various prizes.

## Core Objectives
- **Employee Engagement:** Provide a fun and interactive way for employees to follow sports tournaments.
- **Social Interaction:** Build a community through leaderboards and shared interests.
- **Reward System:** Recognize top predictors with exciting prizes (cash, electronics, etc.).

## Key Games & Use Cases

### 1. Match Outcome Prediction (Core)
The primary game where players predict the result of every match (Home Win, Draw, or Away Win).
- **Points-based system:** Correct predictions earn points.
- **Match Weights:** Important matches (e.g., Finals) have higher point multipliers.
- **Leaderboard:** Real-time rankings based on total points accumulated throughout the tournament.
- **Grand Prizes:** High-value items like iPhones or laptops for top-ranked players.

### 2. Exact Score Betting
A high-reward game focused on predicting the precise final score of specific matches.
- **Cash Rewards:** Correct predictions result in direct monetary payouts.
- **Multiple Bets:** Players can place multiple score bets per match (up to an admin-defined limit).
- **Multipliers:** Rewards can be multiplied for duplicate correct bets or special featured matches.

### 3. Tournament Champion Prediction
A long-term prediction made before or early in the tournament.
- **Goal:** Correctly identify the ultimate winner of the tournament.
- **Prizes:** Exclusive rewards for those who correctly predict the champion.
- **Locking Mechanism:** Predictions are locked once the tournament reaches a certain stage.

## Target Audience
The platform is exclusively for **internal company employees**. It is accessed via internal Single Sign-On (SSO) to ensure a secure and private environment.

## Platforms
- **User App:** A responsive web application optimized for both desktop and mobile devices, allowing employees to place bets and track their progress on the go.
- **Admin Panel:** A comprehensive management interface for administrators to manage tournament data, configure rules, and handle scoring.
