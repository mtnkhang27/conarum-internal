# Getting Started

Welcome to **Conarum Prediction**! This guide will help you set up your account and start making your first predictions.

## 1. Access the Platform
- Open your browser and navigate to the internal URL provided by the IT department.
- Use your company credentials (SSO) to log in.

## 2. Set Up Your Profile
Once logged in, it's a good idea to personalize your profile:
- Go to the **Account** page.
- Choose a **Display Name** (this will be visible on the leaderboard).
- Select your **Favorite Team**.
- Upload an **Avatar** (optional but recommended for a more social experience).

## 3. Understand the Games
There are three main ways to participate:
- **Match Outcome:** Predict if a match will end in a Home Win, Draw, or Away Win to earn points for the leaderboard.
- **Exact Score:** Predict the final score (e.g., 2-1) for specific matches to win cash prizes.
- **Champion Pick:** Select the team you think will win the entire tournament.

## 4. Make Your First Prediction
- Go to the **Available Matches** tab.
- Find an upcoming match.
- Click on your predicted outcome (Home, Draw, or Away).
- Don't forget to click **Submit Predictions** to lock in your picks.

## 5. Track Your Progress
- Visit the **Leaderboard** to see how you rank against your colleagues.
- Check the **My Predictions** page to see your history and the status of your current bets.

## 6. Tips for Success
- **Stay Informed:** Keep an eye on team news and performance to make more accurate predictions.
- **Strategic Weights:** Pay attention to matches with higher multipliers (e.g., Finals) – they can significantly boost your rank.
- **Early Champion Pick:** Make sure to select your tournament champion before the deadline!
