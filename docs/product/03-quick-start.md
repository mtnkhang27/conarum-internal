# Quick Start (5-Minute Guide)

Short on time? Here's how you can make your first prediction in under 5 minutes:

### 1. Log In (1 min)
- Navigate to the **Conarum Prediction** internal portal.
- Sign in with your company credentials.

### 2. Pick a Match (1 min)
- Go to the **Available Matches** tab.
- Find a match that you're interested in.

### 3. Choose Your Outcome (1 min)
- Click on **Home**, **Draw**, or **Away** for the match outcome.
- (Optional) Go to the **Exact Score** tab and enter your predicted score (e.g., 2:1).

### 4. Submit (1 min)
- Click the **Submit Predictions** button to lock in your picks.
- Confirm your submission when prompted.

### 5. Check the Leaderboard (1 min)
- Visit the **Leaderboard** page to see your current ranking.

**Done!** You've officially entered the competition. Good luck!
