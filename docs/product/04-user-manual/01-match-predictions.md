# Match Predictions

The **Match Predictions** page is the central hub for predicting the outcome of every match in the tournament.

## 1. Finding Matches
- Matches are categorized into **Available** (upcoming) and **Completed** (finished).
- You can find matches by date, tournament stage (e.g., Group Stage, Quarter-Finals), or team.

## 2. Placing Your Prediction
- For each available match, you can select one of three outcomes:
  - **Home:** Predicted win for the home team.
  - **Draw:** Predicted tie between both teams.
  - **Away:** Predicted win for the away team.
- Once you make a selection, the outcome will be added to your **Prediction Slip**.

## 3. Submitting Predictions
- To finalize your picks, click the **Submit Predictions** button in the Prediction Slip.
- You can submit multiple predictions at once.
- Once submitted, you can still change your pick until the **Match Kickoff**.

## 4. Scoring
- Points are awarded for each correct prediction.
- The amount of points earned depends on the **Match Weight** (e.g., regular matches are 3 points, finals can be up to 15 points).
- Results and points are updated once the match is officially finished and scored by an administrator.
