# Exact Score Betting

**Exact Score Betting** is a game where you predict the precise final score of a match (e.g., 2:1, 0:0).

## 1. How it Works
- On the **Exact Score** page, you'll see a list of upcoming matches.
- For each match, enter your predicted scores (Home : Away).

## 2. Placing Your Bets
- You can place up to **3** score predictions per match (this limit is admin-configurable).
- You can also bet on the **same score multiple times** (e.g., placing two bets on 2:1).
- If you place multiple bets on the same score and it's correct, your reward will be **multiplied**.

## 3. Rewards
- Correct predictions result in **cash prizes**.
- The base reward for a correct score prediction is **200,000 VND**.
- **Bonus Multipliers** (e.g., 1.5x) may be applied for featured matches or high-stakes games.

## 4. Betting Lock
- Bets are **auto-locked** at the match kickoff.
- In some cases, betting may be locked earlier (e.g., 30 minutes before kickoff).
- Once locked, you can no longer place or change your bets.

## 5. Payouts
- Payouts are processed **24 hours after the match ends**, allowing time for any score corrections or verification.
- Rewards are added to your account balance and can be redeemed as per company policy.
