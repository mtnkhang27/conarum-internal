# Tournament Champion Prediction

Predicting the **Tournament Champion** is a long-term goal that rewards players who correctly identify the ultimate winner of the tournament.

## 1. Finding Teams
- On the **Tournament Champion** page, you'll see a grid of all participating teams.
- Each team card displays the team flag and name.

## 2. Choosing Your Champion
- To pick a team, simply click the **Select** button on the team card.
- A confirmation dialog will appear, asking you to confirm your selection.
- Once confirmed, your champion pick is saved.

## 3. Changing Your Pick
- You can change your champion pick until the **Tournament Champion Lock Date**.
- This date is typically around the start of the tournament.
- Once the lock date passes, no further changes are allowed.

## 4. Winning
- If your chosen team wins the entire tournament, you'll be eligible for exclusive **Grand Prizes**.
- In some cases, multiple people may pick the same correct champion. In this case, prizes may be split or a random draw may occur.

## 5. Rewards
- High-value prizes (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro Max) are awarded to those who correctly identify the champion.
- Winners are announced after the tournament final.
