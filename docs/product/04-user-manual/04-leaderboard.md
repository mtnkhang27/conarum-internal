# Leaderboard

The **Leaderboard** is where you can see your current rank and compare your progress against other players.

## 1. Finding Your Rank
- On the **Leaderboard** page, you'll see your current rank at the top.
- Your rank is based on the total number of points you've earned from all your correct predictions.

## 2. Comparing with Others
- The main leaderboard table shows all players ranked from 1st place downwards.
- For each player, you can see their **Rank**, **Display Name**, **Avatar**, and **Total Points**.
- You can also see additional stats like **Total Correct Predictions**, **Total Predictions**, and **Current/Best Streak**.

## 3. Podium
- The top-3 players are showcased on a **podium** with special badges.
- This highlights the most successful predictors in the tournament.

## 4. Streaks
- The **Current Streak** shows how many consecutive correct predictions you have made.
- The **Best Streak** shows your highest ever streak.
- Streaks are a fun way to measure your prediction accuracy and consistency.

## 5. Updates
- The leaderboard is updated periodically (e.g., every 5 minutes) once matches are officially scored.
- Ranking changes reflect your recent prediction results.

## 6. Prizes
- Top-ranked players on the leaderboard at the end of the tournament will win various **Grand Prizes**.
- Check the project guidelines for a list of prizes and their respective ranks.
