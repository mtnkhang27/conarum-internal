# Profile Management

**Profile Management** allows you to personalize your profile and keep your account details up to date.

## 1. Accessing Your Profile
- Go to the **Account** page in the main navigation.

## 2. Personalizing Your Profile
- You can update your **Display Name**, which is shown on the leaderboard and in other areas of the platform.
- You can also upload a custom **Avatar** (or select from a list of predefined avatars) to represent yourself.
- Select your **Favorite Team** to show your support.

## 3. Account Details
- You can view and edit your **First Name**, **Last Name**, **Email Address**, and other contact information.
- These details are used for prize distribution and communication.

## 4. Bio
- You can write a short **Bio** to tell others more about yourself.
- This is a great way to personalize your profile and connect with other players.

## 5. Saving Changes
- To save any changes to your profile, click the **Update Profile** button.
- Your updated profile will be reflected across the platform immediately.

## 6. Language & Preferences
- In some cases, you may be able to change your **Language** and other application preferences.
- Check the **Account** page for any available settings.
