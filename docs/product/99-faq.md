# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are some common questions and answers about the **Conarum Prediction** platform:

### 1. General Questions
- **Q: Who can participate in the platform?**
  - **A:** The platform is exclusively for current company employees.
- **Q: How do I access the platform?**
  - **A:** You can access the platform via the internal URL using your company SSO credentials.

### 2. Predictions & Betting
- **Q: Can I change my predictions after I submit them?**
  - **A:** Yes, you can change your predictions until the match kickoff. Once the match starts, your predictions are locked.
- **Q: How many predictions can I make per match?**
  - **A:** For match outcome predictions (Home Win, Draw, Away Win), you can only make one pick per match. For exact score betting, you can make up to 3 predictions per match (this limit may vary by match).
- **Q: When is the tournament champion prediction locked?**
  - **A:** The tournament champion prediction is typically locked at the start of the tournament. Check the specific lock date on the **Tournament Champion** page.

### 3. Scoring & Points
- **Q: How are points calculated?**
  - **A:** Points are awarded for each correct prediction based on the **Match Weight**. Regular matches are 3 points, while finals can be up to 15 points.
- **Q: When are results updated?**
  - **A:** Results are updated once a match is officially finished and scored by an administrator. This usually happens within a few hours of the match ending.

### 4. Rewards & Prizes
- **Q: What prizes can I win?**
  - **A:** Prizes vary by tournament and rank. Common prizes include cash, electronics (e.g., iPhones, laptops), and other items.
- **Q: How do I claim my prizes?**
  - **A:** Winners will be notified by the HR or project team at the end of the tournament. Prizes are distributed according to company policy.

### 5. Technical Support
- **Q: What should I do if I encounter a bug or issue?**
  - **A:** Please report any technical issues to the IT support team or the platform administrator.
- **Q: Is the platform mobile-friendly?**
  - **A:** Yes, the platform is designed to be responsive and works on both desktop and mobile browsers.
